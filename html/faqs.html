Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Job Applicant

To apply for a job, you need to create a profile, fill in your details, and upload your resume. Once done, you can browse job postings and apply directly through the platform.

Yes, you can edit your profile at any time. However, make sure to keep your information accurate and up to date.

You can upload your resume in PDF, DOC, or DOCX format. Ensure the file is less than 5MB.

You will receive a confirmation email once your application is successfully submitted. You can also track your application status in your profile.

Yes, you can apply for multiple jobs as long as your qualifications meet the job requirements.

HR

As an HR user, you can manage job postings through the platform’s admin dashboard. You can create, edit, and remove job postings, as well as manage applications.

Interviews can be scheduled directly through the platform. You can select candidates, set interview dates, and notify applicants of their interview schedules.

User accounts can be verified through the verification tools in the admin dashboard. You can check the user’s submitted documents and approve or deny their registration.

Yes, interview feedback can be logged and managed on the platform. This feedback helps in finalizing candidate selection.

Job descriptions can be updated directly from the job posting management section of the admin dashboard.

Client/Partner

You can request manpower by submitting a Manpower Requisition Form through the platform. The HR department will review and process your request.

Yes, you can track the status of your requisition through your dashboard. Updates will be provided by the HR team as your request is processed.

You can update your company’s information, such as contact details and address, in your partner profile settings.

Submitted resumes can be viewed in your dashboard under the job requisition details. You will be able to see all candidates who applied.

Yes, you can communicate directly with HR through the messaging feature in your dashboard.

District Manager

As a District Manager, you can evaluate deployed employees using the evaluation tools available on the platform. You can provide feedback and assess performance based on set criteria.

Yes, you can generate detailed reports on employee performance through the platform. These reports can be used for decision-making and improving operational efficiency.

Employee attendance can be tracked through the platform’s attendance monitoring tools. You can view attendance logs and track trends.

Yes, the platform allows you to provide detailed feedback on employee performance, which can be shared with HR for further actions.

Employee grievances can be submitted and managed through the platform’s grievance management system. You can review and resolve issues as needed.
